ScadaBR

EF (Enhanced Front-end) version, based on ScadaBR 1.1CE

Automation for everyone

From home projects to industrial environment, ScadaBR is the ideal solution. With more than 20 communication protocols, alarm management and support for creation of reports and HMIs, ScadaBR provides an accessible and reliable platform for automation.

ScadaBR Project website

Have freedom

ScadaBR is free and open-source software. Licensed under GNU GPL v3, ScadaBR is a SCADA system without any restrictions on usage time or quantity of Tags / Data Points. Using ScadaBR, your creativity is the only limit.

Need help?

The ScadaBR Forum is the place where the ScadaBR community shares tips, ideas and answers questions. Are you a company or need special paid support? In the ScadaBR Forum you can also have access to ScadaBR 'integrators', people and companies that provide commercial solutions involving ScadaBR.

ScadaBR Forum website

The future of the project

In 2016, a group of developers from outside Brazil started the Scada-LTS project, a ScadaBR fork with new features and the latest technologies. In the future, the current versions of ScadaBR, based on Mango M2M, will be replaced by new versions based on Scada-LTS.

If you are a ScadaBR developer or enthusiast, please consider contributing to Scada-LTS project.
